Biography

Owen Hamlin is an American singer-songwriter and producer based in Los Angeles. From his early collaborations with fellow artists at Berklee College of Music to a mentorship under rock ‘n’ roll legend Mike Pinera, Owen Hamlin has consistently showcased a rare blend of creative independence and collaborative versatility. Whether in the studio or on stage, Owen Hamlin delivers a powerful connection that cuts through the noise. He’s not following trends — he’s building a legacy.
