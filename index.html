Biography

Owen Hamlin is an emerging American singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and producer based in Los Angeles. With a growing reputation for electrifying live performances and coverage from tastemaker outlets, he’s quickly becoming one to watch. Owen’s music is defined by its raw emotional depth and the seamless fusion of electric and acoustic sounds. His creative process centers on capturing the spark of initial inspiration—resulting in songs that reflect an honest and unfiltered artistic vision.
